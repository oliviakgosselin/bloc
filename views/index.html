Why Bloc

We’re lovers of cities and the diverse networks that they foster. You can find us hanging out at bookstores, parks, coffee shops and wine bars, getting to know the people and places around us. We believe there’s no better way to live ones life than experience all the idiosyncrasies that make places great. Whether you’re hanging out in Italian Market in Philadelphia, playing soccer in Williamsburg’s McCarron park or just taking in the views of DowntownLA from the arts district
